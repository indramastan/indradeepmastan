A hearty Welcome!

I have finished my Ph.D. from CSE @ IIT Gandhinagar, under the guidance of Dr. Shanmuganathan Raman. I worked on Image Restoration and Image Synthesis using Deep Learning. Prior to Ph.D, I had wonderful experiences serving Hiddenreflex Bangalore and developing Epic Privacy Browser. Presently, I am working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineer at LNMIIT Jaipur.

I spent my most beautiful and crucial phase of life at IISC Bangalore. I pursued my M.E. from Computer Science and Automation Department @IISC. I did my thesis in the domain of financial cryptography, under the guidance of Dr. C. E. Veni Madhavan. Also worked closely in the software engineering field as part of my undergraduate projects in MNIT jaipur.

Apart from working on academic projects, I enjoy dancing, sketching, cooking, and beatboxing. I have participated in multiple stage performances in my student life.
